Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your pet's first visit to Shiny Paws.

Appointments are strongly recommended. We do offer same-day service when availability allows, and emergency grooming within one hour for urgent needs. Book directly through our website's checkout page.
We specialize in hand scissor grooming for Poodles, Goldendoodles, Pomeranians, and Maltese — but we welcome all breeds and sizes of dogs and cats. Every pet receives personalized, gentle care.
We use exclusively premium organic shampoos and conditioners that are gentle on sensitive skin and safe for all coat types. No harsh chemicals or synthetic fragrances — ever.
Timing varies by service and breed. A basic Bath & Brush typically takes 1–2 hours, while a Full Grooming session may take 2–4 hours. We'll always provide an estimate when you book.
Absolutely. Your satisfaction and your pet's happiness are our top priority. If you're not 100% happy with the result, let us know and we'll make it right. Free estimates are provided within 2 days of inquiry.
